Principles

Three ideas, every screen.

01

Quiet surface, loud signal

Deep, near-black navy holds the stage so the data — files, jobs, moments in a clip — can actually speak. Color earns its way in.

02

Engineered, not decorated

Monospaced stamps on metadata. Tight tracking on headings. Every pixel works for the operator staring at a pipeline at 2am.

03

One family, three dialects

Akomi, Tsunami, and The Watcher each get their own accent color — but the neutrals, type, and components are shared.
